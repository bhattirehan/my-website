What Is Kaolin Clay (China Clay)?

Understanding Kaolin Clay

Kaolin Clay, commonly referred to as China Clay, is a soft, white clay rich in the mineral kaolinite. It’s widely used across industries such as

  • Paper: for coating and filler.
  • Ceramics: for porcelain and fine ceramics.
  • Paints and coatings: for brightness and opacity.
  • Cosmetics and pharmaceuticals: in skincare and oral care products.
